Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you request a consultation, Mijutalk collects the following personal information:

  • Required: Name, phone number, area of consultation, available time slots
  • Optional: Email address, estimated financial assets, detailed inquiry

2. Purpose of Collection and Use

  • To provide financial consultation and support services
  • To respond to inquiries and improve our service quality
  • To perform internal analysis and marketing (only with prior consent)

3. Retention Period

Personal information is retained for 6 months after the consultation is completed, then securely deleted.
In certain cases, data may be retained longer as required by law.

4. Third-Party Sharing & International Transfers

Mijutalk does not share personal information with third parties unless:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Consent has been explicitly provided by the user

Data may be stored on servers located in South Korea and accessed from the United States.
Transfers are handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including CCPA.

5. User Rights

  • Access and correct your personal information
  • Request deletion or restriction of processing
  • California residents may exercise their rights under the CCPA

Requests: privacy@mijutalk.co

6. Safeguards

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Access control and logging
  • Internal data protection training
  • Physical security measures for document storage

7. Data Protection Officer

Name: Sanghyun Lee
Phone: +82-70-7139-1635
Email: privacy@mijutalk.co

8. Cookies & Analytics

We use Google Analytics to improve our services and analyze traffic anonymously.
No personally identifiable information is linked with this data.
Users can opt-out using: Google Analytics Opt-Out Tool

9. Notification of Changes

This policy may change based on legal or service updates.
Changes will be announced on our website at least 7 days in advance.
