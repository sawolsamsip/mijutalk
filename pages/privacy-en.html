Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you request a consultation, Mijutalk collects the following information:

  • Required: Name, phone number, consultation topic, available time
  • Optional: Email, financial assets, details of inquiry

2. Purpose of Use

  • To provide financial consulting and support
  • To respond to inquiries and improve services
  • For internal analytics and marketing (with prior consent)

3. Retention Period

Your information will be stored for 6 months after consultation and then securely deleted, unless longer retention is required by law.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

  • Only when required by law or with your consent
  • Information may be stored on servers in Korea and accessed from the US

5. Your Rights

  • Request to view, correct, delete or limit processing of your data
  • Email: privacy@mijutalk.co

6. Safeguards

  • SSL encryption
  • Access controls and logging
  • Staff training
  • Locked physical storage
